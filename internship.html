Admission

Graduate Schools in Waseda University accept applications from international students. You can find the accurate and latest information here:

https://www.waseda.jp/fsci/en/admissions_gs/

If you are interested in research at my group, I am happy to support your application. Please remember that, since Waseda University is the private university, the fees are not low. It is your responsibility to secure the fees.

Internship

While I do not open an official internship programme, I am happy to host you as an internship student at my group. If you are interested, please send an email to me. However, in order to distinguish your important request email from spams, I would like you to first ask your supervisor to write to me (especially if we do not yet know each other), so that I can understand who you are. I tend to not reply to a direct mail from a sender who I do not know. Also, please understand that, depending on situation, I cannot always accept your request.
